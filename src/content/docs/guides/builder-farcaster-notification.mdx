## Overview

[Propdate](./src/content/docs/guides/builder-propdates.mdx) notifications can be received through the existing [Farcaster bot](https://github.com/BuilderOSS/builder-farcaster) by following the bot account on Warpcast.

## Tutorial

1. Go to Warpcast.
2. Follow the account: https://warpcast.com/builderbot
3. Receive propdate updates in your DMs.
